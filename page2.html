SQUID vs OCTOPUS

SQUID

the squid has 8 arms and 2 tentacles, the difference being arms have suckers all the way down and tentacles only have suckers at the ends

OCTOPUS

Octopus, unlike squids, only have 8 arms and no tentacles. the difference being arms have suckers all the way down and tentacles only have suckers at the ends

Lifespan


the longest octopus life span is the giant pacific at 3-5 years. octopus die shortly after their eggs hatch meaning their lives are generally short

Habitat


salt water oceans, octopus have been found at depths ranging from shallow tide pools to up to 7000m bellow sea level

diet


clams, shelfish,seastars and fish

size

the largest octopus, the giant pacific octopus, can weigh up to 71 kg


the smallest octopus, the wolfi octopus, weighs less then a gram
